what ti is konekt?

details

Tkonekt is IT community that gathers IT experts, junior and senior IT professionals, experienced IT managers, so as students in IT and interns. We want to use great possibilities of IT networking, that leads to new projects, great profit and unlimited opportunities of career development for IT professionals. ITkonekt brings together the whole IT scene of Serbia in one place. Among ITkonekt's partners are 50 IT companies from Serbia and region, 30 IT organizations and educational institutions and more than 6000 IT experts. Once a year the whole IT scene gathers at the ITkonekt event, the biggest event in Serbia and region. ITkonekt also includes educational workshops in IT, meetups in companies, IT experts' blog and other activities.
ITkonekt will have three parts: ITjobfair, ITconference and GameSpace.
